What is GIS?


GIS stands for Geographic Information Systems/Science.


GIS lets us visualize, question, analyze, and interpret spatial data
to understand relationships, patterns, and trends.


GIS has been applied in many different disciplines for problem-solving through a spatial lens,

e.g., biology, anthropology, criminology, earth science, linguistics, history, economics, business,
sociology, justice studies, etc.
